# ResilientRider

ResilientRider is an AI-powered insurance and financial protection platform designed for gig economy delivery riders. The platform addresses common challenges faced by independent riders, including financial instability, lack of insurance coverage, delayed claim processing, and limited access to emergency credit.

The system combines intelligent risk assessment, dynamic insurance pricing, fraud-aware claims processing, micro-loan services, real-time rider tracking, and behavioral analytics into a single integrated platform. By leveraging artificial intelligence and data-driven decision-making, ResilientRider helps improve rider safety, financial security, and operational efficiency.

## Problem Statement

Gig workers often operate without the benefits available to traditional employees. Most riders lack adequate insurance coverage, face difficulties accessing emergency financial support, and have limited protection against income disruptions caused by accidents, weather conditions, or unforeseen circumstances.

ResilientRider was developed to provide a technology-driven solution that offers personalized insurance, intelligent claim verification, financial assistance, and proactive risk management.

## Key Features

* AI-based rider risk assessment during registration
* Dynamic insurance premium calculation based on rider behavior and environmental factors
* Intelligent claims processing with fraud detection
* Weather-verified parametric claim validation
* Behavioral learning engine for rider profiling
* Emergency micro-loan approval system
* Real-time GPS tracking and rider monitoring
* Instant notifications using Socket.IO
* Secure payment processing through Razorpay
* Administrative dashboard with analytics and risk monitoring

## Technical Architecture

The platform follows a modern full-stack architecture consisting of a React-based frontend, a Node.js and Express backend, MongoDB Atlas for data persistence, and multiple AI-driven modules responsible for pricing, claims analysis, policy intelligence, and risk prediction.
